📝 About Us

Rotaract Club of Hong Kong Financial Centre is a youth-led service organization under Rotary International. Established by passionate young leaders, we are committed to creating positive change through community service, professional development, and cultural exchange.

  • ✨ Serve the local and global community
  • 💬 Develop leadership and soft skills
  • 🌏 Connect with like-minded individuals across Hong Kong and beyond

From social impact projects and fundraising campaigns to personal growth workshops and fellowship events, we strive to live out the Rotaract motto:
"Fellowship Through Service."

  • 📍 Based in Hong Kong
  • 💼 Sponsored by the Rotary Club of Hong Kong Financial Centre
  • 🎯 Open to all passionate individuals aged 18–30

Join us to grow, lead, and serve — together.
